# ADR: Orchestrator: mandatory commit and document steps

**Status**: proposed
**ID**: c70493a6
**Proyecto**: [[projects/lemoria/README|lemoria]]

## Description

Restructured orchestrator workflow from 8 steps to 13 steps. Steps 9 (Commit) and 10 (Document) are now MANDATORY after any implementation. Added Closing Checklist with 6 verification items that must pass before reporting to user. Updated documentation-agent with vault sync and what-to-document table. Updated github-agent with git status check and empty-commit guard.

## Rationale

Keep optional delegation (current behavior), Add pre-commit hook instead
